1. Bold
    • Use Case: Specifies strong importance or emphasis.
    • Best Practices: Use strong for semantic importance, and b for stylistic bolding.
  2. Italic
    • Use Case: Specifies emphasized text.
    • Best Practices: Use em for semantic emphasis, and i for stylistic italics.
  3. Strikethrough
    • Use Case: Represents text that has been struck through.
    • Best Practices: Use s, strike, or del for semantic strikethrough based on the context.
